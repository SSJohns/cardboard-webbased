Cardboard Web Demo

A test using Google Cardboard based in the web. To make a workable room
viewer that allows users to make their own 3D rooms out of panoramic images



  1. Go to upload and pick an image to make into a VR room
  2. A link will be generatd to the equivalent room at the top of the page
  3. Go to the link on your phone and share it with your friends
